3. Blazor Server UI Design

Home Page: Shows an overview of appointments, upcoming visits, and recent medical records.

Patient Management Page: Displays a list of patients with CRUD operations (Create, Read, Update, Delete).

Doctor Management Page: Allows management of doctors' information and specialties.

Appointment Scheduling Page: Allows users to schedule and manage appointments between patients and doctors.

Medical History Page: Displays medical records and allows adding/updating patient histories.
